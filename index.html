About Shree Om Dairy Farm

Since 1991, Shree Om Dairy Farm has proudly served Vasai with premium dairy delights. From farm-fresh milk to handcrafted sweets, we bring purity, taste, and authenticity to every product.

Our dairy follows a hygienic and efficient cold-chain process, ensuring quality and freshness in every sip and bite. All ingredients are sourced locally and monitored for excellence.
