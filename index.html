⭐ About Shree Om Dairy Farm

Since 1991, Shree Om Dairy Farm has proudly served customers with a wide range of premium dairy delights. From traditional favorites to innovative milk-based creations, we combine authentic taste with uncompromised quality across all our in-house products.

We are more than just a dairy brand — we are a legacy of purity, taste, and trust passed down through generations.


🥇 What Makes Our Products Unique?

  • 🌿 Authentic Recipes: Prepared with age-old techniques for genuine flavor
  • 🧪 No Preservatives: Freshness without compromise
  • 🧊 Cold Chain Maintained: Ensures taste, safety, and longer shelf life
  • 🏠 Made In-House: From milk to mithai, every item is crafted at our own facility
  • 🎯 Daily Quality Checks: Rigid standards followed every single day

🏭 Our Manufacturing Process

  • 💧 Fresh, High-Quality Milk sourced daily
  • 🧼 Hygienic Preparation Spaces and automated processes
  • 📦 Proper Packaging Protocols for freshness and safety
  • ❄️ Cold Storage and Transportation to maintain quality till the last mile
